⭐ Final Year Project in progress

Deepfake News
Detection System

A collaborative research project tackling one of the most critical challenges of our digital era — AI-generated misinformation. The system detects both synthetic text articles and manipulated video/image media.

Objective

Build a hybrid detection pipeline combining NLP transformers for fake news text classification with computer vision for video/image deepfake detection — delivering a reliable end-to-end misinformation flagging system.

Tech Stack

Deep learning with TensorFlow/PyTorch, transformer models (BERT family), OpenCV for media analysis, and a Python-based backend serving real-time predictions.
